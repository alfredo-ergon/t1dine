# Privacy and Data Classification

## Restricted health data
Glucose values, insulin settings, dose calculations, insulin-on-board values, meal history linked to a person, device identifiers linked to health records, caregiver relationships, and Nightscout data.

## Confidential account data
Identity claims, contact details, support interactions, consent records, and export/deletion requests.

## Public or licensed food data
Food records are still governed by source licences, attribution, territory, and update obligations.

## Rules
- Collect the minimum required data.
- Separate operational telemetry from health records.
- Redact before logging, not after ingestion.
- Provide account deletion, health-data deletion, and portable export.
- Make retention periods explicit.
- Keep local-first or device-only options where practical.
- Do not transmit food photos or labels without explicit disclosure and purpose.
- Child and caregiver models require jurisdiction-specific review.
