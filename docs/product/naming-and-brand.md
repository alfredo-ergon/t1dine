# Naming and Brand

## Working name
T1Dine

## Working descriptor
Smarter carb counting for life with Type 1.

## Transition option
T1Dine, from Comer com DT1

## Brand constraints
- Do not imply diagnosis, cure, guaranteed control, or autonomous treatment.
- The Core product name should remain food-oriented.
- Dose Assist naming and claims require separate clinical and regulatory review.
- Complete trademark, company, app-store, domain, and social-handle clearance in target markets.
- Test pronunciation and unwanted meanings across launch languages.

The working name is not an asset until clearance is complete.
