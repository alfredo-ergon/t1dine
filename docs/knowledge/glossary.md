# Glossary

- Available carbohydrate: carbohydrate definition excluding fibre according to the applicable source convention.
- Canonical food: product record used for search and meal construction while preserving source observations.
- Correction factor: configured glucose reduction associated with one unit of insulin.
- FPU: fat-protein unit, an optional concept that must not silently alter a dose.
- Insulin-to-carbohydrate ratio: grams of carbohydrate covered by one unit of insulin.
- IOB: insulin on board or active insulin.
- Market: country or commercial territory where a packaged or restaurant product definition applies.
- Provenance: source, version, licence, market, method, and transformation history supporting a value.
- T1D: Type 1 diabetes.
