# Food Data Quality Policy

## Quality dimensions
- source authority;
- recency;
- market match;
- nutrient completeness;
- unit and basis clarity;
- preparation-state match;
- internal plausibility;
- cross-source consistency;
- human verification;
- user confirmation history.

## Conflict policy
Keep conflicting observations. Select a preferred observation through explicit rules based on market, source authority, effective date, preparation state, and user context. Never use a silent mean.

## AI and OCR
AI or OCR output is a draft. Show extracted fields, source image, uncertainty, and required confirmation. Do not promote it automatically to trusted canonical data.

## Search display
Users should see a concise confidence indicator and be able to inspect the source. Do not overwhelm the primary meal journey with database jargon.
