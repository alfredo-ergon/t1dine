# First 12 Weeks

## Weeks 1 to 2
Asset audit, user interviews, product boundary, parity validation, initial hazard workshop, source licensing review, brand screening, and architecture decisions.

## Weeks 3 to 4
Executable monorepo, CI, synthetic fixtures, canonical schema, provenance contracts, mobile shell, API shell, local database, and telemetry redaction tests.

## Weeks 5 to 6
Offline search slice using one authoritative Portugal dataset and a controlled packaged-product subset. Build admin source review and quality reporting.

## Weeks 7 to 8
Meal builder, quantities, nutrition totals, favourites, recent meals, and local persistence. Conduct accessibility and usability testing.

## Weeks 9 to 10
Recipes, custom foods, history, account sync, export/deletion, and conflict handling.

## Weeks 11 to 12
Barcode workflow, Nightscout sandbox integration, beta hardening, migration prototype, store disclosure draft, and release readiness review.

Dose Assist during these weeks is limited to intended-use decisions, hazards, requirements, prototypes, and verification planning. Claiming production readiness would be dishonest.
