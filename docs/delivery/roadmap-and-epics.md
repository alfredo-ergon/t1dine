# Roadmap and Epics

## Phase 0: Ownership and discovery
- Existing-app asset and licence audit.
- User research and workflow observation.
- Intended-use and regulatory discovery.
- Brand clearance.

## Phase 1: Foundation
- Monorepo, environments, identity baseline, privacy model, telemetry redaction, canonical food schema, source register, and offline data contracts.

## Phase 2: Core MVP
- Onboarding and profile.
- Search and food detail.
- Barcode workflow.
- Meal builder and nutrition totals.
- Favourites, recents, custom foods, recipes, and history.
- Offline sync.
- Portugal-first migration source, then selected launch-market sources.

## Phase 3: Data operations and integrations
- Admin curation.
- Scheduled source ingestion.
- Search quality and localisation.
- Nightscout integration.
- Export, deletion, caregiver controls, and support tooling.

## Phase 4: Assisted entry
- Label OCR and photo-assisted candidate generation.
- User confirmation, uncertainty, and correction learning that never changes clinical settings.

## Separate Dose Assist workstream
- Intended use and regulatory strategy.
- Clinical governance and hazards.
- Deterministic engine and independent verification.
- Human factors and usability validation.
- Quality system, release evidence, and post-market plan.

The Core beta and Dose Assist release must not be treated as the same milestone.
