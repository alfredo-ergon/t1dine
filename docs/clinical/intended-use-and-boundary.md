# Intended Use and Product Boundary

## T1Dine Core
The initial intended use is nutritional information, meal construction, food history, and user-controlled data exchange. Core must not claim to diagnose, prescribe, optimise therapy, or autonomously recommend insulin.

## Dose Assist candidate boundary
Dose Assist may calculate a transparent estimate from explicitly entered or synchronised inputs and clinician-approved profile parameters. Whether that output can be labelled a recommendation is a regulatory and clinical decision, not a copywriting decision.

## Required decisions before release
- target jurisdictions;
- adult, adolescent, child, and caregiver populations;
- prescription or non-prescription status;
- supported insulin types and delivery methods;
- fingerstick, CGM, or both;
- insulin-on-board source and mandatory status;
- trend handling;
- exercise, illness, alcohol, and other adjustments;
- clinician provisioning and review of settings;
- maximum dose and hypo thresholds;
- residual-risk ownership;
- post-market surveillance and incident handling.

A disclaimer does not neutralise the intended functionality.
